Why A Catio?

Protect Your Cat

from vehicles, predators, poisons, diseases from other animals or getting lost. Like dogs and small children, cats who are let outdoors without supervision are vulnerable to the dangers of cars, other animals, cruel people, and diseases. Many people consider free-roaming cats to be pests. They do not want cats to urinate, defecate, dig, eat plants, or kill birds on their property. Across the country, free-roaming cats are shot, poisoned, and stolen by angry neighbors. They are also mutilated, drowned, beaten, set on fire, used in ritual sacrifice, or used by dogfighters as “bait.”

Reduce Vet Bills

They are also highly contagious and can easily be passed on to other companion animals.
